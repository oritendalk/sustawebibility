Recipes for a Sustainable Future!

Self Care

DIY Hair Masks

Combine 2 tablespoons of coconut oil with 1 tablespoon of honey for a natural deep conditioner. Apply to damp hair and leave for 30 minutes before rinsing.

Homemade Soap Recipe

Mix olive oil, coconut oil, and lye to create your own eco-friendly soap. Add essential oils like lavender or citrus for fragrance.

Sustainable Skincare

Try making your own exfoliating scrub with sugar, honey, and a splash of lemon juice for glowing skin.

DIY Makeup Tips

Create your own natural lip balm with beeswax, coconut oil, and a hint of beetroot powder for color.

Reduce

  • Use a reusable water bottle instead of single-use plastics.
  • Opt for digital receipts instead of paper ones.
  • Plan meals to minimize food waste at home.

Reuse

Thrifting Tips

Check out local thrift stores for second-hand clothing, furniture, and other items. Repurpose old items to give them new life! If you want to know where some are, check out our maps section!

Recycle

Recycling Cans and Bottles

Clean and dry aluminum cans and plastic bottles before recycling. Check your local recycling rules for sorting.

Recycling Paper and Cardboard

Break down cardboard boxes and avoid recycling wet or greasy paper, like pizza boxes.

Recycling Plastics

Recycle plastics labeled with numbers 1 and 2, and reuse others where possible.

Recycling Metals

Scrap metal can often be taken to specialized recycling centers for cash!

Recycling Batteries

Drop off used batteries at collection sites or recycling facilities to avoid environmental harm.

Recycling Other Items

Check your local waste management services for information on recycling unusual items like electronics or glass.
